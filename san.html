Wear it! Corona viruses are spread through droplets created when you cough, sneeze, and even talk. Even if you don't have any visible symptoms, you could still be carrying the virus and risking spreading it to others. Wearing a mask that snuggly covers your nose and mouth helps reduce these types of transmissions.
